Two Families, One Story

The left side of the periodic table holds the most reactive metals. Groups 1 & 2 share a love for losing electrons, but each group dances to its own beat.

Group 1

Alkali Metals

  • Valence Shell: ns¬π
  • Charge Tendency: +1 cations
  • Texture: Soft, easily cut
  • Reactivity: Explosive with water
Group 2

Alkaline Earth Metals

  • Valence Shell: ns¬≤
  • Charge Tendency: +2 cations
  • Texture: Harder, structural
  • Reactivity: Controlled but assertive
Shared Motifs

Periodic Trends

  • Electronegativity falls as you move down.
  • Atomic radius expands with each period.
  • Ionization energy drops, fueling reactivity.
